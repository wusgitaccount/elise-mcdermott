Summer Utopia
210x297mm
Publication

841x1189mm
Poster series

In my project I explored the idea of vision and reflection. By doing this I visualised the use of mirrors and how objects/ideas can be seen differently through different angles, which was inspired by the book Ways of Seeing by John Burgess. He made many statements that inspired me to work on a project surrounding visual interpretations of a dream world. I used mirrors as a guiding concept within my project.

I wanted it to be a series that included a publication, posters and animation.

I started by creating a mood board and art direction on what I envisioned the images to look like. I collaborated with a film photographer, Aislyn Stevens, who used a film camera to take the images while I also took digital images to compare the two outcomes. I ended up using a mix of both film and digital photographs to create my publication and designs.

Publication as a result it was interesting to look at the juxtaposition of analogue and digital photography. I think using both and contrasting between the two was a succesful result. I noticed the colours and texture differ between the images and it was fun to experiment combining the two with my collages.

Posters I wanted to explore mixed media, so I created these images by printing the photgraphs as a3 prints and then by using paint and glitter, I distorted the images by ripping up the images and scanned them through and finally blew it up to an a0 size to print.

Animation Using After effects and Photoshop I created animations with the collages I created to compliment my work.
