elise mcdermott

If we consider this virtual space assigned to my name, Elise McDermott, to represent me, then it should seem only natural that I have devoted it to a project that has inhabited my mind for countless, consecutive days. What I have been preoccupied with is the notion of re-complexifying the design of modern recipes. With this website, a site of deconstruction under constant construction, I will organize my thoughts and research on recipes and imagine new forms of them.
